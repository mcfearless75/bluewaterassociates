## 2. Membership Tiers

Skool has one paid membership price per community on Hobby. Use the **Courses** space to gate content by progress/level, and the **Groups** (subgroups) feature (Pro only) for tier separation.

### Phase 1 (Hobby plan — months 1–4)

| Tier | Skool Setup | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | Open join, limited access to Classroom | £0 |
| Founding Member | Paid join, full Classroom + Community | £29/mo (launch offer, then £79) |

> Skool Hobby only supports one paid price. Set it to £29 for the founding member launch window, then increase to £79 once you close founding access.

### Phase 2 (Pro plan — month 5+)

| Tier | Skool Setup | Price |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Free | Waitlist / welcome only | £0 |
| Member | Full community + Pillars 1 & 2 | £79/mo |
| Pro | Member + live sessions + resources | £149/mo |

> Cohort (£1,995) and 1:1 (£150–250) are invoiced outside Skool via Stripe or Bluewater Associates invoicing.

## 6. Community Rules (post in pinned + About section)

```
Cipher Commons — Community Rules

1. Be specific. Vague questions get vague answers.
2. No self-promotion without context — share your work only if it adds value to the thread.
3. No sharing of SANS, GIAC, ACFE, or AICPA copyrighted material.
4. Treat every member as a professional. No condescension.
5. Spotted an error in the course material? Flag it in the relevant category — we'll fix it.
6. Keep firm/client details out of public posts. Use DMs for sensitive case chat.
7. Spam = immediate removal.
```
