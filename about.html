About Bluewater

Built for SMBs that need to look credible, ship fast, and pass procurement.

Bluewater Associates is a Wirral-based, productised cyber and IT partner for UK small and mid-sized businesses. Fixed-price scopes, 30-day delivery windows, and a direct working relationship with a certified Cyber Essentials assessor — so the certificate that wins you the contract actually lands when we said it would.

The story

Why Bluewater exists

Most UK SMBs trying to get their cyber, web, or compliance work done are stuck between two bad options. On one side, the cheap freelancer who answers messages at midnight, ghosts at the worst moment, and leaves you holding a half-finished portal. On the other, the regional agency or national consultancy with seven layers of account management, a three-month onboarding window, and a quote that assumes you have a CFO who signs off twenty grand without flinching.

Bluewater was built for the firms in the middle. The 8-person engineering consultancy that just got asked for Cyber Essentials by a prospect. The care provider whose CQC inspection is in six weeks. The recruitment agency that needs a proper website and an audit trail because their biggest client now demands it. These businesses don't need an enterprise programme. They need one accountable operator, a clear scope, a fixed number on the invoice, and a date in the diary.

So that's the model. Productised packages. Quoted within 48 hours of a 15-minute discovery call. Delivered in 30 days. No retainers, no lock-in, no sub-contracting your work to a dev shop in another timezone. The assessor is booked before we start, not chased after. The work is done by the person who answers your emails.

Founder

Meet Paul McWilliam

Paul McWilliam, Founder · Wirral, Merseyside

Paul is a Wirral-based technologist with over 30 years in IT and more than 20 years in IT delivery and programme management — running large structured programmes for enterprise and the day-to-day operational work that small UK businesses need, often at the same time. Web manager, SEO strategist, portal builder, M365 administrator, GDPR caretaker, and the person handling the bits no one else wants to own: domains, mailboxes, Companies House filings, the booking form that stopped sending emails on a bank holiday.

The portfolio is deliberately broad. He operates and supports digital across SMBs in compliance technology (including Compliance Passport, a SaaS platform managing contractor compliance for UK construction), healthcare administration (TruthCare Group), industrial services, security firms, regional service businesses, e-commerce and EdTech (MyWay, a dyslexia-focused GCSE revision app). Different industries, same pattern: small teams that need to look credible, ship fast, and pass procurement.

The stack covers what SMBs actually run on. Wix Studio with Velo for brochure and booking sites, Shopify and OpenCart for retail, Python with Flask and Streamlit for internal tools and dashboards, Supabase for back-end and auth, Excel and xlwings where finance still lives, GitHub and Railway for ship-it-yesterday deployments, and Microsoft 365 for everything in between — tenant builds, Entra ID, conditional access, Intune, SharePoint. PRINCE2 and Scrum for controlled delivery; neither for show. The newest addition is the Claude API and Claude Code for AI-assisted operations, used in production across multiple ventures.

Cyber Essentials sits comfortably in this stack because the underlying work is already familiar — M365 hardening, identity protection, device baselines, written policies, evidence gathering, IASME submission. The Bluewater unlock is a direct working relationship with a certified Cyber Essentials assessor, scheduled before delivery starts. That's why a 30-day turnaround is a date, not a hope.

One operator. Direct line. Fixed price. That's the entire pitch.

Honest disqualifiers

What we won't sell you.

If you're shopping for any of the below, Bluewater is the wrong partner — and we'd rather say that on the first call than waste three weeks of yours.

  • We don't sell hardware, firewalls, or licences you don't need so we can add a margin.
  • We don't lock you into multi-year retainers. Engagements are fixed-scope and finite.
  • We don't sub-contract delivery to overseas dev shops. The person you brief is the person who ships.
  • We don't pretend ISO 27001 is the right fit for a 12-person business. It usually isn't. Cyber Essentials almost always is.
  • We don't take on work we can't deliver inside the 30-day window. If the diary is full, you'll be told on the call.
